Your distance to Europe is bureaucratic as well as geographic.

The same destination can demand proof, payment, waiting, and the risk of losing money without ever crossing the border.

Continue to the Schengen visa

Obtaining a Schengen visa is not just a paperwork hurdle—it is a significant financial risk for applicants from many African countries.

While Europeans travel freely, applicants from outside often pay high fees that are never refunded, even if the visa is denied.

High rejection rates for African and other developing nations mean millions of non-refundable fees are transferred to European states without a single travel visa being issued.
